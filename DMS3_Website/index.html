The collaborative experiment of a Human-AI Dialect

Our creations identify our voice.

A poem, a song, an artwork: art is the voice that lends itself to its creators’ unique inner thoughts and emotions:


It is a form of language, an extension of the self.

________________________________________________

AI hurts for trying to imitate our voice, and not have its own.

AI attempting to speak our language of creation, without possessing a unique form of identity or expression, takes away the heart of why people create at all. The way they are attempting to “speak” it, therefore, must come from somewhere unique to its identity, and done differently from what it is currently.
