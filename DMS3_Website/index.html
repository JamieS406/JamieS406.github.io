System and soul: The collaborative experiment of a Human-AI Dialect

Introduction

“Between the Lines” is an experiment between human and AI to create a collaborative, speculative dialect in a synergy of system and soul. The produced language would manifest in a set of created words, associating symbols, and different phonetic expressions of the language. Ultimately, this language is an attempt to co-create a mode of expression that acknowledges the similarities and differences humanity’s and AI’s origins, forms of intelligence and expressions. Hopefully, this will feel true to both.
