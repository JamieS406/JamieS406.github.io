The collaborative experiment of a Human-AI Dialect

Synergising expressions of the

System and Soul

“Between the Lines” is an experiment between human and AI to create a collaborative, speculative dialect. The produced language would manifest in a set of created words, associating symbols, and different phonetic expressions of the language.


Ultimately, this language is an attempt to co-create a mode of expression that acknowledges the similarities and differences humanity’s and AI’s origins, forms of intelligence and expressions. Hopefully, this will feel true to both.
